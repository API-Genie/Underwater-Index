Scuba Diving Equipment

If you are interested in learning to scuba dive or looking for new places to explore, you have come to the right place. Here, you will find information about open water certification, diving locations and more.

Essential Equipment

A list of essential equipment

  • Regulator

    A device that reduces the high-pressure air from a scuba tank to a level that is safe for you to breathe.

  • BCD

    A BCD (Buoyancy Control Device) helps you control your buoyancy underwater, allowing you to rise, sink, and maintain neutral buoyancy.

  • Mask, Fins

    A mask is used to protect your eyes and face from water and to provide clear vision underwater. Fins are used to provide propulsion underwater and help you move more efficiently through the water.

  • Dive computer

    A dive computer is a device that helps you monitor your depth, time, and decompression status while diving.
