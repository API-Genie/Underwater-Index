Open Water Scuba Certification

What is Open Water Scuba Certification?

Open Water Scuba Certification is the entry-level scuba diving certification that is recognized worldwide. It is designed to give you the basic knowledge and skills you need to dive safely and comfortably with a buddy in open water environments. The certification process involves taking a class, completing pool dives and open water dives, and passing a final exam.

Certification Providers
